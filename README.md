Introduction (Expert Perspective)
From a prompt engineering standpoint, the quality of an AI chatbot’s responses
depends heavily on how instructions are framed. In customer support systems,
prompts must reduce ambiguity, guide the AI toward structured reasoning, and ensure
clarity for non-technical users. Each prompting technique serves a specific interaction
goal—speed, clarity, accuracy, or context gathering.
1. Straightforward Prompts
Expert Explanation
Straightforward prompts provide clear, concise instructions to the AI. They are ideal for
direct answers, definitions, or short explanations.
Example Prompts & AI Outputs
1. Prompt:
"Define solar energy in one sentence."
AI Output:
"Solar energy is the energy harnessed from sunlight and converted into electricity or
heat."
2. Prompt:
"List three benefits of wind power."
AI Output:
"1. Reduces greenhouse gas emissions
2. Provides sustainable and renewable energy
3. Creates job opportunities in the renewable sector"
Use Case Insight:
 Quick and direct answers for student-friendly summaries.
 Ideal for FAQs or introductory explanations.

3. Preceding Question Prompting
Expert Explanation
Preceding question prompting engages the AI to first ask clarifying questions or
provide reasoning steps before giving the final answer. Useful for complex or multistep explanations.
Example Prompts & AI Outputs
1. Prompt:
"Why is renewable energy important for climate change mitigation? Explain the
mechanisms."
AI Output:
"Renewable energy is important because it reduces reliance on fossil fuels. Could you
specify if you want an explanation for electricity generation, transportation, or industrial
use?
 Electricity: Using solar and wind reduces CO₂ emissions.
 Transportation: Biofuels and electric vehicles reduce greenhouse gas output.
 Industry: Geothermal and hydro reduce carbon footprint.
Overall, renewable energy lowers global warming potential by decreasing
greenhouse gases."
2. Prompt:
"How does hydropower generate electricity? Describe simply."
AI Output:
"To explain clearly, let’s break it down:
1. Water is stored in a dam or river.
2. Flowing water turns turbines.
3. Turbines spin generators.
4. Electricity is produced and transmitted to homes.
Do you want me to also explain its environmental impacts?"
Use Case Insight:
 Enhances context-aware responses.
 Improves accuracy for ambiguous questions.
4. Missing Word Prompting
Expert Explanation
Missing word prompting provides partial sentences, guiding the AI to complete
answers. Useful for practice exercises or controlled responses.
Example Prompts & AI Outputs
1. Prompt:
"The main greenhouse gas emitted by burning fossil fuels is ____."
AI Output:
"Carbon dioxide (CO₂ )."
2. Prompt:
"Wind turbines convert ____ energy into electrical energy."
AI Output:
"Mechanical (kinetic) energy."
3. Prompt:
"Hydropower relies on the movement of ____ to generate electricity."
AI Output:
"Water."
Use Case Insight:
 Excellent for educational quizzes or training AI to give concise answers.
 Ensures accuracy and consistency in structured learning.
